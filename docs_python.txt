## server.py —— 主服务器
- 1-20: imports, load_names()从names.txt读100个姓名, NAME_POOL, pick_random_name()
- 20-25: TOTAL_MAPS=6, PROGRESS_MAX_MAP={1:10,2:10,3:14,4:14,5:18,6:20}, SEMESTER_NAMES
- 25-100: 游戏状态管理 (init/get/save, build_grid, build_lines, reveal, bad_ending, check_progress_full, transition)
- 100-200: API路由 (/api/state 含pre-exam分支, /api/enter, /api/choose)
- 200-210: / 路由 → 直接读取返回 game.html
- 210+: __main__

## modules/map_gen.py —— 地图生成
- GRID_SIZE_MAP, REENTERABLE_TYPES
- randint, shuffle, bfs_distance
- generate_connections(grid_size) —— 中心边只增不删
- ensure_type_diversity(map_data, conn, center_key)
- generate_map(map_count) —— 确保中心至少连通一个学习节点

## modules/events.py —— 事件节点（HTML同步版）

- LEARNING_OPTIONS (含_narrative), EXTRACURRICULAR_POOL/FIXED, get_extracurricular_options()
- TEST_OPTIONS (含_narrative), RESEARCH_OPTIONS (含_narrative)
- CONFESSION_OPTIONS (含_narrative, _is_confession), DATING_COMBOS (含_narrative)
- DREAM_OPTIONS (含_narrative), EXAM_PREP_OPTIONS (含_narrative)
- ENCOUNTER_EVENTS (含narrative), IDLE_OPTION (无所事事)
- REENTERABLE_TYPES, BLOCKABLE_TYPES
- 所有选项已精简为1-3个状态变化
- get_options_for_type(cell_type, character) → 奇遇返回[随机事件, IDLE_OPTION]
